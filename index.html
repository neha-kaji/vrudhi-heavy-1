About Us

Established in 2004, Vrudhi Heavy Engineers has become a leader in the manufacture and supply of premium components for massive industrial machines. Based in Shiroli MIDC, Maharashtra, we serve clients across construction, mining, and heavy transport sectors. Our team is dedicated to combining innovation with industrial-strength durability to ensure our clients operate with the best.

With over 100 employees and 25,000 sq ft of manufacturing space, we deliver precision-engineered components with the highest quality standards. Our certifications include ISO 9001:2015 and ASME accreditation.

ISO 1990 Certification

At Vrudhi Heavy Engineers, our adherence to ISO 1990 demonstrates our unwavering commitment to producing components that meet stringent international benchmarks for offshore applications. This certification assures our clients of our capability to deliver products that withstand the harsh marine environment, maintain structural integrity under extreme conditions, and contribute to the overall safety and efficiency of offshore operations. It reflects our dedication to continuous improvement and our expertise in specialized heavy engineering for critical infrastructure.
